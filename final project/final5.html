Tourist Attractions in Changzhou

  • Tianning Temple

    Tianning Temple, also referred to as Tianning Monastery, is a majestic Buddhist temple situated in Changzhou, Jiangsu province. With a rich history dating back over a thousand years, it stands as a testament to Buddhist art, architecture, and spiritual practice.

    The temple is renowned for its grandeur and impressive structures. At its center stands the Tianning Pagoda, a towering architectural masterpiece that dominates the skyline. Rising to a height of over 150 meters, it is one of the tallest pagodas in the world.

    The pagoda, with its elegant design and intricate ornamentation, showcases the exquisite craftsmanship of ancient Chinese artisans. It offers visitors panoramic views of the surrounding cityscape from its observation deck, providing a breathtaking experience.

  • Yancheng Safari Park

    Yancheng Safari Park is a remarkable wildlife park situated in Chanzghou, Jiangsu province. Spanning over a vast area, it provides a unique opportunity to observe and interact with a wide variety of animal species in a natural habitat setting.

    The park is designed to offer visitors a memorable and educational experience. As you explore the park, you can encounter magnificent creatures from around the world, including lions, tigers, elephants, giraffes, zebras, and many more.

    One of the highlights of Yancheng Safari Park is its open drive-through safari area. Here, visitors can embark on a thrilling safari adventure, driving their own vehicles or taking guided tours, while animals roam freely around them.
